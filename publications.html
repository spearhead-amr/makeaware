Publications

Publications and presentations produced within MAKEAWARE! are collected on this page, reflecting the project’s contributions to design research, healthcare, and public engagement. These outputs build on co-created data, hands-on workshops, and digital tools to make AMR understandable and relatable, encouraging responsible antibiotic use and engaging citizens in the fight against Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR).
