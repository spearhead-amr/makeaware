OpenHealth Datathon

Antimicrobial resistance is a global threat that directly or indirectly affects each and every one of us.
On the 15th of March, people who experienced antimicrobial resistance, together with healthcare professionals, will be working supported by designers to envision possible technology solutions to the challenge of resistance development.

The event

Antimicrobial resistance is a global threat that directly or indirectly affects each and every one of us. Join us for the OpenHealth datathon at the Novartis Pavillion, in Basel, for a day of discovery, exchange and contribution to the research. A "datathon" is an event where participants gather to solve practical problems through tools and techniques based on data, by working together in teams to generate insights and potential solutions. Data might not only be numerical but stories and experiences are also important information to consider when diseases involve human beings.
During the datathon, people will be asked to share their experience and knowledge to imagine and prototype new technological tools to help citizens and doctors face situations and consequences of antimicrobial resistance.
