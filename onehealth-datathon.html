OpenHealth datathon: Designing future patient-led conversations

Antimicrobial resistance is a global threat that directly or indirectly affects each and every one of us. On the 21st of June at Novartis Pavillon come and share your expertise together with people who experienced antimicrobial resistance, healthcare professionals, designers and technologists to envision possible technology solutions to the challenge of resistance development.

The event

OpenHealth Datathon is a half-day event open to all. Participants gather to work together in teams, through tools and techniques based on data, to imagine and prototype future solutions to help citizens and doctors face situations and consequences of antimicrobial resistance. The event is organised by the MAKEAWARE! initiative, part of the Spearhead project and supported by Innosuisse.
